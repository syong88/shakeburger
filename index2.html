  • Stand For Something Good

  • We Stand For Something Good® in everything we do. That means carefully sourced premium ingredients from like-minded purveyors we admire and love; thoughtful, well-crafted and responsible design for its place; and deep community support through donations, events and volunteering. Thanks for standing with us!

Good Ingredients

We pride ourselves on sourcing incredible ingredients from like-minded ranchers, farmers, bakers and food purveyors. Our burgers are made with 100% all-natural Angus beef—vegetarian fed, humanely raised and source verified. The Chick’n Shack™ is 100% all-natural cage-free chicken. Our flat-top dogs? 100% all-natural Vienna beef. Our fries are crinkle cut Yukon potatoes, with zero artificial ingredients. And our vanilla and chocolate frozen custard recipes use only real sugar, no corn syrup and milk from dairy farmers who pledge not to use artificial growth hormones.

  • PAT LAFRIEDA

    Our proprietary blend of freshly ground (never frozen!) 100% Angus beef, made from premium whole muscle cuts—no hormones or antibiotics, EVER—is the founding DNA of our menu. We work with Pat LaFrieda and other amazing butchers throughout the U.S.

  • U.S. ANIMAL WELFARE POLICY

    To further transparency for our team, guests and suppliers, our U.S. Animal Welfare Policy outlines our core beliefs, where we are and where we’re going.

  • NIMAN RANCH

    The SmokeShack® is topped with 100% gluten-free, MSG-free, nitrite-free bacon, made using old world charcuterie techniques by Niman Ranch‘s independent family farmers, who raise livestock humanely and sustainably.

  • STUMPTOWN COFFEE

    Our custom, organic Shack coffee blend is roasted in Brooklyn by Stumptown, sourced Direct Trade from farms in Central and South America and Indonesia.

  • BROOKLYN BREWERY

    A burger tastes better with a cold craft beer. ShackMeister® Ale, made just for us by Brooklyn Brewery, is a brew worthy of our eats with its deep golden color, bright citrus aroma, slight bitterness and bready malt flavor.

  • MARTIN'S POTATO ROLLS

    The best thing since sliced bread: all buns at all Shacks, worldwide, are non-GMO, made by our potatoey pals at Martin’s Potato Rolls.

  • VIENNA BEEF

    Shake Shack began as a humble hot dog cart. Our dogs are 100% all-natural Vienna beef with no hormones and no antibiotics, EVER.

  • FROZEN CUSTARD

    Our vanilla and chocolate recipes use only real sugar, no corn syrup and milk from dairy farmers who pledge not to use artificial growth hormones. Equally sweet: Our fudge sauce and caramel sauce have zero shelf-life lengtheners.

  • CAGE-FREE EGGS

    Our breakfast sandwiches feature cage-free eggs sourced from local U.S. family producers, whose hens are fed an all-vegetarian diet. We also use cage-free eggs for our buttermilk herb mayo.

  • BOCCE'S BAKERY

    ShackBurger dog biscuits are baked by hand just for us by Brooklyn-based Bocce’s Bakery. They’re all-natural, preservative- and chemical-free. Woof!

    Meet Brooklyn Brewmaster, Oxford Companion to Beer editor-in-chief and Shack BFF Garrett Oliver.

Good 'n' Green

We pride ourselves on sourcing incredible ingredients from like-minded ranchers, farmers, bakers and food purveyors. Our burgers are made with 100% all-natural Angus beef—vegetarian fed, humanely raised and source verified. The Chick’n Shack™ is 100% all-natural cage-free chicken. Our flat-top dogs? 100% all-natural Vienna beef. Our fries are crinkle cut Yukon potatoes, with zero artificial ingredients. And our vanilla and chocolate frozen custard recipes use only real sugar, no corn syrup and milk from dairy farmers who pledge not to use artificial growth hormones.

  • SHACK2O®

    We’re a proud member of 1% for the Planet, which means 1% of every SHACK2O® bottle sold supports the Waterkeeper Alliance and the cleanup of water sources around the globe.

  • SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE

    Through partners like Niman Ranch, we’re committed to supporting ranches and farms designed to have minimal negative impact on the environment, preserving the land for future generations.

  • POST-COLLECTION RECYCLING

    You sort! We sort! All bottles, cardboard, plastics and cooking oil get 100% sorted post-collection.

  • OIL MANAGEMENT

    Thanks to our partnership with Restaurant Technologies Inc., 24,384 pounds of trash have been eliminated, or the equivalent of what 124 dumpsters can hold, as a direct result of having a clean oil management system.

  • SUSTAINABLE FORESTRY INITIATIVE

    Our paper disposables are made from Sustainable Forestry Initiative (SFI)-certified paper and printed with soy ink.

  • SHACK SWAG

    Much of the gear sold in the Shake Shack Shop is made from organic cotton and/or post-consumer recycled polyester.

  • MAST BROTHERS CHOCOLATE

    Our exclusive Mast Brothers Shake Shack Buttermilk Chocolate Bar is an exclusive blend of premium cacao beans sourced directly from small farm cooperatives around the world, served in a recycled heavyweight paper wrapper.

  • SOLAR ROOFS

    Raise the roof! Our King of Prussia, PA; The Domain, Austin; and Westbury, Long Island Shacks feature solar roofs that each produce about 20,400kWh/year, or the average consumption of two average-size single homes.

  • SHIFT_DESIGN

    This Philly-based design group is the mastermind behind the planters at many of our Shacks—and the Center City, Philly Shack’s green roof and eco-friendly bike corral.

Good Neighbors

We strive to be the best employers and citizens of each neighborhood we call home. Every Shack donates 5% of sales of a specific concrete to a local charity partner, and our Shack Gives Back volunteer program encourages our teams to get involved in causes they feel passionate about—from animal welfare to education—in their communities.

  • Shack Gives Back

  • Our teams donate more than 2,000 hours a year to serve meals to the hungry, mentor kids in need, clean up parks and much more, all for the betterment of their Shack’s community.

    Community lies at our core. The five main areas we support are: hunger relief, arts and culture, education and family, environment, animal welfare and health.

  • Great American Shake Sale™

    Giving is sweet! Every year since 2012, we’ve teamed up with Share Our Strength’s No Kid Hungry® campaign to raise money towards their goal of ending childhood hunger in America. During the campaign, Shake Shack guests who donate $2 or more receive a card for a free shake—valued at $6—redeemable on their next visit.

    One hundred percent of donations benefit No Kid Hungry. To date, we’ve donated more than a million dollars, thanks to YOU!
